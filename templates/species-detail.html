Boerhavia Species Information

Boerhavia erecta (Safed Punarnava)

Hindi Name: सफेद पुनर्नवा
Boerhavia erecta, commonly known as "Safed Punarnava," is a valuable medicinal plant. It is primarily used to treat kidney and urinary disorders. It acts as a diuretic, helping to reduce water retention in the body and is effective for managing joint pain and arthritis. This species is known for its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, aiding in the treatment of various inflammatory conditions.

Boerhavia diffusa (Lal Punarnava)

Hindi Name: लाल पुनर्नवा / रक्त पुनर्नवा
Boerhavia diffusa, known as "Lal Punarnava" or "Rakta Punarnava," is a rejuvenating herb widely used in traditional medicine. It is renowned for its effectiveness in treating liver disorders, respiratory conditions, and detoxifying the body. It also possesses anti-cancer properties and is a potent antioxidant. This species is beneficial in improving digestion and managing diabetes due to its adaptogenic properties.
